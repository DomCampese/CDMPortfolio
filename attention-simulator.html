Attention Simulator

The attention simulator is the first major project I developed in the course where I leveraged the p5.js javascript library. The finished product is an interactive animation that I created so that I could visually represent our attention when considering it as a limited resource. when designing the experience, I chose busy and crowded images that lead the user to explore the space and find themself frustrated by the limited and fleeting attention they are given by the varying blur and grayscale effect. The idea of attention and its fleeting nature relates back to this central idea of human experience; most students and professionals struggle with knowing where to divide their attention in this busy, attention grabbing society in which we operate.
