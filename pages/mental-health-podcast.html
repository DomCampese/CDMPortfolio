Talk It Out - Mental Health Podcast

Talk it out is my project for the audio medium where I envisioned a fictional podcast where I talk to college students of varied backgrounds and career paths to give them a chance to express themselves and to listen to and understand their experiences and opinions. For the episode I created about work-life balance in school, I interviewed three of my roomates; Joey, Andrew, and Jack to get an idea of how each of us view our workloads and mental state during the semester. Going in to the podcast, I expected my roomates to mirror my sentiments regarding work life balance; that there is essentially too much work and too little free time. Interestingly, I found that two of my roomates have found ways to maximize thier free time and in turn, quality of life during the semester. I found the podcast to be an opportunity for listening, learning, and self-reflection.
