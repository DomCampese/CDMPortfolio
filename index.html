Composing Digital Media Portfolio

This website serves as a portfolio showcasing the digital media that I produced in Composing Digital Media in the fall semester of 2021 at the University of Pittsburgh. Each week, we developed a new relevant skill in digital creation and applied it with either a making assignment or a larger, more involved project. Throughout the semester, I developed four projects, each aligning with a different medium: image, audio, game, and video. The common thread linking each of my projects together is the idea of the human experience, which I explored through the lense of our perceptions in each these different media.
